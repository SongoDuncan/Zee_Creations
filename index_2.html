Our story

Made with hands, not machines.

ZEE's Homemade Creations started with one pot, one stove and a love of good scent. Every candle is still poured the same way today — in small batches, tested by hand, and finished with a wick that's trimmed just right.

No shortcuts, no mass production — just candles made the way we'd want to burn them ourselves.
